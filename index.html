About Our Dispensary

Welcome to Alaska Cannabis Exchange, Anchorage's trusted destination for high-quality cannabis products. Located conveniently at 2906 Spenard Rd, we pride ourselves on offering a welcoming atmosphere where both new and experienced consumers can find exactly what they need.

Our knowledgeable budtenders are passionate about cannabis education and helping you find the perfect product for your needs. Whether you're looking for pain relief, relaxation, or recreational enjoyment, we've got you covered.

We carefully curate our selection to include only the finest flowers, edibles, concentrates, and accessories from Alaska's top growers and manufacturers. Every product in our store meets our strict standards for quality, purity, and potency.
