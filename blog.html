Understanding Cannabis Products: A Complete Guide

Welcome to Alaska Cannabis Exchange's comprehensive guide to understanding cannabis products. Whether you're new to cannabis or looking to expand your knowledge, this guide will help you navigate the various options available at our dispensary.

1. Cannabis Flower

The most traditional form of cannabis, flower refers to the dried buds of the cannabis plant. At Alaska Cannabis Exchange, we carry three main types:

Indica

Known for its relaxing effects, Indica strains are often used in the evening or for pain relief. Common effects include:

  • Full-body relaxation
  • Reduced pain and inflammation
  • Increased appetite
  • Help with insomnia

Sativa

Sativa strains tend to provide more energizing effects, making them popular for daytime use:

  • Cerebral, uplifting effects
  • Increased creativity and focus
  • Reduced anxiety and stress
  • Social engagement

2. Cannabis Concentrates

Concentrates are potent extracts that contain high levels of cannabinoids. Our dispensary offers several types:

Shatter

A glass-like concentrate that "shatters" when broken. It's one of the purest forms with THC levels often exceeding 80%.

Wax

With a consistency like candle wax, this concentrate is easier to handle than shatter and offers similar potency.

Live Resin

Made from fresh-frozen cannabis plants, preserving more terpenes for enhanced flavor and aroma.

3. Edibles

Cannabis-infused foods and drinks provide an alternative to smoking. Important considerations:

  • Start with low doses (5-10mg THC)
  • Effects take 30-90 minutes to appear
  • Effects last 4-8 hours
  • Store properly to maintain freshness

4. Topicals

Cannabis-infused lotions, balms, and oils that are applied directly to the skin. Benefits include:

  • Localized pain relief
  • Reduced inflammation
  • No psychoactive effects (unless they contain THC)
  • Great for arthritis and muscle soreness

Choosing the Right Product

Our knowledgeable budtenders can help you select the perfect product based on:

  • Your desired effects
  • Experience level
  • Medical needs
  • Preferred consumption method
