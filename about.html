Our Story

Rooted in Alaska, growing with our community

From Humble Beginnings

Founded in 2016, Alaska Cannabis Exchange began as a small dispensary with a big vision - to provide Anchorage with the highest quality cannabis products while fostering education and responsible consumption.

What started as a single storefront on Spenard Road has grown into Anchorage's premier cannabis destination, thanks to our loyal customers and dedicated team.

Our founders, longtime Alaskan residents and cannabis advocates, saw an opportunity to create a dispensary that combined professional retail standards with deep product knowledge and community values.

Meet Our Founder

Aaron Ralph

Founder / Owner

Aaron's journey began in California, growing up in a family that prioritized exploration over possessions. By age 12, he had traveled to every U.S. state, developing a deep connection to nature and self-reliance. In 1984, his family's travels brought them to Alaska—and it has been his home ever since.

For nearly two decades, Aaron has been a passionate advocate for cannabis reform, dedicating his knowledge and energy to educating individuals, healthcare professionals, and policymakers about its benefits. His commitment to wellness, community, and plant-based healing led him to launch Alaska Cannabis Exchange in 2015, inspired by his best friend Geneva Cowen.

Despite the challenges—including Geneva’s heartbreaking passing—Aaron has grown ACE into a trusted, respected name in Alaska's cannabis community. His mission remains rooted in education, access, and compassion.
