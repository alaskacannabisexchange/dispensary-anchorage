Privacy Policy

At Alaska Cannabis Exchange, we are committed to protecting your personal information and being transparent about how we handle it. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices regarding data collection, usage, and protection when you interact with our website and services.

Last Updated: January 1, 2023

By accessing our website, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy.

Information Collection

We may collect certain personal information to provide you with better services and comply with legal requirements:

  • Contact Details: Name, email address, phone number when you reach out to us
  • Location Data: ZIP code or general area to verify service eligibility
  • Age Verification: Confirmation of your legal age to access cannabis products
  • Usage Information: Browser type, IP address, pages visited, and interaction patterns
  • Preferences: Product interests or wishlist items (where applicable)

Note: We never collect or store payment information through our website.

Age Restrictions

Our services are strictly for adults aged 21 or older. We implement age verification measures and:

  • Do not knowingly collect information from underage individuals
  • Promptly delete any information if we discover it was provided by someone under 21
  • Require age confirmation before granting access to certain content

How We Use Your Information

Your data helps us provide quality service while complying with Alaska state regulations:

  • Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
  • Send service-related communications (with your consent)
  • Enhance website functionality and user experience
  • Maintain compliance with cannabis industry regulations
  • Verify legal age and location requirements
  • Analyze website usage to improve our offerings

We do not sell your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

Data Protection Measures

We implement robust security practices to safeguard your information:

  • SSL encryption for all data transmissions
  • Secure hosting with regular security updates
  • Limited employee access to sensitive information
  • Regular security audits and monitoring

While we take extensive precautions, no online platform can guarantee absolute security. We encourage users to practice safe browsing habits.

Cookies and Tracking

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Remember your preferences and age verification status
  • Analyze traffic patterns and popular content
  • Improve site performance and functionality

You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings, though this may affect certain features.

Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary for:

  • Fulfilling the purposes outlined in this policy
  • Complying with legal and regulatory requirements
  • Maintaining business records as required by law

When no longer needed, data is securely deleted or anonymized.

Information Sharing

We may disclose information in limited circumstances:

  • With service providers who assist in website operations (under confidentiality agreements)
  • When required by law or to protect our legal rights
  • With analytics providers to understand website usage

We never share your data with unrelated third parties for their marketing purposes.

Your Rights and Choices

You have certain rights regarding your personal information:

  • Request access to the data we hold about you
  • Ask for corrections to inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal data (where applicable)
  • Opt-out of marketing communications at any time
  • Disable cookies through your browser settings

Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to external sites such as:

  • Social media platforms
  • Industry resources
  • Government websites

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites and encourage you to review their policies separately.

Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect:

  • Changes in our practices
  • New legal requirements
  • Improvements in data protection standards

Significant changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
